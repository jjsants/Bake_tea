In the 70's

Hello! My name is Edivar and I founded BakedToATea after taking a break from my career as a Banker. Having discovered a passion for baking and cake art, I developed my techniques making cakes for willing friends and family, before establishing my business in the 70's. BakedToATea has, I am proud to say, since flourished into a thriving boutique home wedding cake design studio, where taste, design and service are upheld in equal measure.

In the 90's

At the heart of BakedToATea is a reputation for delicious cake, with my unique and interesting flavour combinations baked with the best of ingredients sure to create a talking point for guests. My love for cake flavours is shared through recipes on my family, which has been supporting me, contribuiting and helping the company to grow over the years. From this point we also offer delicious complements for weddings or simply a great moment for special moments in people's lives.

In the 2000's

Nowadays we care so much about you that, When it comes to design, my ethos is to work closely with each one of you, drawing on inspiration from each wedding details to create a cake that perfectly compliments your day. Elegant, modern and botanical, my style typically incorporates exquisite details such as bridal lace or gold leaf, and my signature handcrafted sugar flowers. BakedToATea transcend the traditional to the level of delectably edible art.
